Unseen 2020 - ARG

The Begining (Patch Notes)

On the 20th of October, we recieved the first cipher of 2020. It came on the Patch Notes for the 1.4.3. It came with the message:

This year, Dark Harvest begins on October 26 — and there’s more to enjoy than ever before. Brush up on your Unseen glyphs and keep an eye on your inbox. We’re keeping the event shrouded in mystery for now, but details are coming soon!

Beside this we also got a Unseen Cipher

ΣЭЭ­ YΩU­ ­ИN ­TㅎЭ­ ­TㅎЭ ­ΣΠㅏCЭ ­БЭTWЭЭN­ ­ㄹЭㅏΛㅁΣ, ­ΣΛㅏYЭㄹΣ.

When you translate this you get will get this message: See­ you­ ­in ­the­ ­the ­space ­between­ ­realms, ­slayers.
What the Unseen could be refereing to could be the Umbral Void. Which we have seen that Riftstalker most likely live in aswell as Thrax.

Event Portals

On the 23th of October, DauntlessLion posted a video, it was about the Dark Harvest for this year and it contained a lot of new cosmetics and events things.

During a section of the video Lion encounteres a portal

This portal when interacted with, transports the user into a sorta mirror dimension where you can collect these tokens called "Harvest Coins" which Lion shows that you can exchange for cosmetics only available during the Dark Harvest

Shrowd Effigy

Also in this video there was shown a statue that could be found in the hunt areas. Some of you may remember that the same statue was seen in old Ramsgate during the last Unseen Event.


In these videos we are able to see that these statues can be damaged and when they are destroyed they will either spawn a Shrowd Shadow (Clone) or it will spawn a Shrowd Orb. These statues seems to be randomly generated and the effect might be too.

Enigmatic, Unsettling and Mysterious Cipher

Enigmatic Cipher


He also showed of three ciphers which was contained in a lore pieces. The first Cipher goes as follows:

ㅏㄹㄹΩΓㅏNT VЭΣΣЭΛ БㄹΩUΓㅎT ΛΩW ФИNㅏΛ ΠΩㄹT ΩФ CㅏΛΛ, TㅎЭ VΩИΔ ΔㄹY ΣㅋY, ΔㄹYЭㄹ, ЭㅏㄹTㅎ, БㄹΩㅋЭN WИNΓΣ ΓㄹΩUNΔЭΔ

When you translate this you get will get this message: Arrogant vessel brought low final port of call, the void dry sky, dryer, earth, broken wings grounded.
The word dry seems to be here a lot which could refere to the desert area. Though it doesn't leave us much of a hint.

Unsettling Cipher

The second cipher goes as follows:

ㅋNИVЭΣ ΩФ ИCЭ, БΛㅏΔЭΣ ΩФ CΩΛΔ ΔㅏΓΓЭㄹΣ ФㄹΩЗЭN ИN TㅎЭ ㅏCT ΣTΩㄹㅁΣ CㅏNNΩT ΣЭㅏΛ TㅎЭ VΩИΔ ΠㅏㄹㅏΔΩX

When you translate this you get will get this message: Knives of ice, blades of cold daggers frozen in the act storms cannot seal the void paradox
This could refere to the ice area in Dauntless, since the words "ice", "cold", and "frozen" all appear in the text.

Mysterious Cipher

The third and final cipher goes as follows:

ΔΩΩㅁЭΔ ㅏNΔ ΣЭVЭㄹЭΔ, YЭT ИΣ ΣTㅏNΔΣ ㅏΛΛ TㅎㅏT ㄹЭㅁㅏИNΣ, ㄹΩTTИNΓ ㅏNΔ ㅎΩΛΛΩW TㅎЭ VΩИΔ ΣЭЭㅋΣ TㄹㅏΓЭΔY ㄹЭㅁNㅏNT

When you translate this you get will get this message: Doomed and severed, yet is stands all that remains, rotting and hollow the void seeks tragedy remnant
This one does seem to refere to something that was destroyed and it not used anymore. It could be refereing to the old Ramsgate which was destroyed and abandond.

Not to long after the video from Lion was posted another video from CyberMessiah was posted to YouTube where he shows us some new information

In here we get to see two new ciphers that Lion didn't show us, which was the Uncanny and Disturbing Cipher

Disturbing Cipher

Again we have nothing that would lead us to make a concret solution

Dark Harvest returns with Unseen Mysteries and shadowy challenges

Later the same day we got the official post about the Dark Harvest. Which came with some clues to the ciphers that was found in the lore pieces.
And I quote:

Each day from October 26 to October 30, you can track down a new Unseen lore node in Ramsgate that’ll offer clues leading to the location of their portals.

What was also said is that the Shrowd Effigies can drop certain items, but they also have a chance to conjure something far more lethal
