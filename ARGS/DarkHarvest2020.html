The Begining (Patch Notes)

On the 20th of October, we recieved the first cipher of 2020. It came on the Patch Notes for the 1.4.3. It came with the message:

This year, Dark Harvest begins on October 26 — and there’s more to enjoy than ever before. Brush up on your Unseen glyphs and keep an eye on your inbox. We’re keeping the event shrouded in mystery for now, but details are coming soon!

Beside this we also got a Unseen Cipher

When you translate this you get will get this message: See­ you­ ­in ­the­ ­the ­space ­between­ ­realms, ­slayers.
What the Unseen could be refereing to could be the Umbral Void. Which we have seen that Riftstalker most likely live in aswell as Thrax.

Event Portals

On the 23th of October, DauntlessLion posted a video, it was about the Dark Harvest for this year and it contained a lot of new cosmetics and events things.

During a section of the video Lion encounteres a portal

This portal when interacted with, transports the user into a sorta mirror dimension where you can collect these tokens called "Harvest Coins" which Lion shows that you can exchange for cosmetics only available during the Dark Harvest

Shrowd Effigy

Also in this video there was shown a statue that could be found in the hunt areas. Some of you may remember that the same statue was seen in old Ramsgate during the last Unseen Event.


In these videos we are able to see that these statues can be damaged and when they are destroyed they will either spawn a Shrowd Shadow (Clone) or it will spawn a Shrowd Orb. These statues seems to be randomly generated and the effect might be too.

The Second Day of the Dark Harvest

On the second day of the Dark Harvest we got the "Mysterious" cipher which if our theory is true this one should be on the Aulric's Refuge/Dyadic Drift maps.

The reason for it being here is because the cipher says "Doomed and severed" refering to the tree that once stood there, it is now "rotting" as the cipher is saying and therefore the portal is here.

Along with this clue, the Unseen twitter account sent out a pair of ciphers.
